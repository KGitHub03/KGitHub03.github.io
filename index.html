About the Club

Purpose
The purpose of this club is to provide Shaker High School students with the opportunity to learn about the unique applications of programming when it is united with electrical components and mechanical systems. We also want to share our passion of mechatronics and electronics with the students.
Vision
We want this club to be welcoming and inviting for aspiring engineers. This club will allow them to create unique projects to learn the basics. We hope this club opens studentsâ€™ minds to the limitless possibilities of the mechatronics.
Content
We will cover topics in electronics such as knowing how and when to use electrical components such as LEDs, ultrasonic sensors, and much more. A programming language called C++ will be used, which is similar to Java (used in Robotics), and it complements the computer science courses that are offered.
Impact
Mechatronics club will not only demonstrate the possibilities of this field, but also enable them to engineer their own solutions to their own problems. Students will be able to view difficulties from a different perspective and use the appropriate resources to find the most effective solution.
